About Us:

At Maishabora Rehabilitation Center, we are committed to making a difference in the lives of children with special needs. Our team of dedicated professionals is here to help your child reach their full potential and lead a fulfilling life.

Background of Maishabora Rehabilitation Centre

Our Founding Story
Maishabora Rehabilitation Centre was founded in 2020 by three passionate occupational therapists who shared a common vision: to improve the quality of life for children with special needs in Tanzania. Having worked in different settings and seen the daily challenges faced by children with developmental and neurological conditions, they decided to establish a centre that would provide accessible and high-quality therapy services tailored to each child's unique needs. 

Our Purpose 
The centre was created with the primary goal of offering professional, family-centered care for children with special needs, including autism spectrum disorder, cerebral palsy, speech and language delays, sensory processing disorders, Down syndrome, and other developmental challenges. At Maishabora, we believe that early intervention and consistent support can make a life-changing difference. 

Our Growth Journey 
Since its establishment, Maishabora has grown steadily both in its capacity and in the range of services provided. What started as a small initiative has now become a trusted facility that serves children and families from across Dar-es-Salaam and surrounding regions. We continue to expand our programs, develop new partnerships, and strengthen our impact through community awareness and education.

Our Values
  We are guided by the values of empathy, respect, inclusion, and professional excellence. We treat every child as an individual, with their own strengths and potential, and we strive to create a safe and supportive space where children can thrive. 

Our Impact
 Over the past few years, Maishabora has made a measurable impact on the lives of many children. Through personalized therapy programs, children have learned to communicate better, improve motor coordination, gain independence in daily routines, and build social relationships. Families often report a renewed sense of hope and confidence in their children's futures. Looking Ahead 

Our Vision

To establish as a trusted national rehabilitation institution dedicated to enhancing the independence of children with special needs through the delivery of high-quality rehabilitation services.
